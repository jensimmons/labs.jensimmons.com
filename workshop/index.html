About The Flags

  • To turn on Grid in Firefox or Firefox Developer Edition, go to about:config, search for layout.css.grid.enabled, and click on false to turn it to true. You don't need to this in Firefox Nightly, because Grid is already turned on.
  • To turn on Grid in any version of Chrome or Opera, go to chrome://flags/#enable-experimental-web-platform-features or opera://flags/#enable-experimental-web-platform-features and click "Enable".
  • Safari does not have any flags (at least not yet.) There's no way to test in regular Safari. Safari Technical Preview already has Grid turned on.
